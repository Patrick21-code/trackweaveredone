The Method

How TrackWeave works

We treat every discography as a living knowledge graph. Natural language processing extracts lyrical themes, sonic references, and narrative arcs — then draws edges between songs to reveal a richer musical story.

01

Ingest & Parse

Lyrics, metadata, and audio features are ingested for every track. Our NLP pipeline tokenizes, embeds, and extracts semantic entities — themes, emotions, and motifs — from raw text.

NLP Extraction
02

Build the Graph

Each song becomes a node. Edges form between tracks that share lyrical themes, referenced artists, sonic similarities, or narrative continuity — assembling a rich relational network per artist.

Explore & Share
