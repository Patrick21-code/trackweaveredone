Join Our Community

Connect with fellow
music lovers

TrackWeave is more than just a platform — it's a community of passionate music listeners. Join us in building something meaningful together through engagement, feedback, and shared discoveries.

Follow & Connect

Create an account and start following other music enthusiasts. Share your listening history and discover new connections through the community's collective musical journey.

Provide Feedback

Help us improve by sharing your thoughts, reporting bugs, or suggesting new features. Your input directly shapes the future of TrackWeave and makes it better for everyone.

Explore & Discover

Use TrackWeave regularly to explore artist connections and discover new music. The more the platform is used, the richer the graph becomes for the entire community.

Ways to Support

Help us grow through
community action

Your voice, your enthusiasm, and your time are the most valuable currencies for a project like TrackWeave. Here's how you can make a real difference.

Share TrackWeave

Tell a friend who loves music. Post about an artist graph you found interesting. Share a review you wrote. Word of mouth is the most powerful thing a small project has, and every share genuinely matters to us.

Leave a Review

Write a thoughtful review of an album you know well. Your annotations genuinely improve the platform — they help build a richer understanding of music for the entire community.

Share on Social Media

Tag us on Instagram, X, or TikTok when you discover something surprising in a graph. Seeing real listeners react to the music connections TrackWeave surfaces is the fuel that keeps the team going.

Invite a Friend

TrackWeave is more meaningful with people you know. Invite a friend whose music taste you admire and start following each other's listening history. A bigger community means a richer graph for everyone.

A Note From Us

From the team,
with gratitude

We started TrackWeave because we were frustrated. Frustrated that the tools we had for listening to music were incredible at the moment of play, but completely blind to everything that made a discography meaningful — the recurring themes, the callbacks, the evolution of an artist's voice across a decade of work.

Whether you share a graph on social media, write thoughtful reviews, or simply use TrackWeave to discover new music — you are the reason TrackWeave exists. Thank you, sincerely, for being part of this community.
